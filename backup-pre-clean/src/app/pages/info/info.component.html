Overview

Your daily points are split into Obligation and Growth. The app also computes a Daily Gauge (0–150) that compares your day to sensible targets, and adds Weekly Bonuses for consistency.

Obligation vs Growth

  • Obligation: Fardh prayers (0–5). Witr can be counted as obligation (on by default).
  • Growth: Nafl count, Duha, Qiyam, Qurʾān pages, Dhikr counts, Fasting, Sadaqah.
  • The top card shows Obligation, Growth, and the overall Grand Total.

Daily Gauge (0–150)

The gauge normalizes your day against friendly targets. 100 ≈ targets met. Above 100 means you went beyond.

Targets used for the gauge
  • Fardh: 5
  • Nafl: 12
  • Duha: done
  • Qiyam: done
  • Qurʾān: 4 pages (no hard cap; just a target)
  • Dhikr: 100 per item (Astaghfirullah, Ṣalawāt, etc.)
  • Fasting: depends on day (see below)
  • Sadaqah: money + kindness
Colors
  • Green ≥ 100: met/exceeded targets
  • Yellow 60–99: solid progress
  • Grey < 60: room to grow

Fasting (context-aware)

The label and points depend on the day:

  • White Days (13–15): “White Days fasting” — higher weight.
  • Mondays/Thursdays: “Sunnah fasting” — medium weight.
  • Other days: “Optional fasting” — light weight.

All future days are locked. Past rules apply to the day’s type in your current month.

Qurʾān & Dhikr

  • Qurʾān: 1 point per page. No max. The gauge uses 4 pages as the daily target.
  • Dhikr: Each item is a counter (0–100). Each rep adds a small value (default 0.1). Past 100, points grow gently via diminishing returns (still rewarded, less linear).

Diminishing returns (gentle)

Extra effort beyond targets still earns points, but with a softer slope. This keeps the score balanced so one area (e.g., very high dhikr) doesn’t drown out others.

Weekly bonuses

  • +10 for ≥ 5 days in a week with gauge ≥ 60.
  • +10 if all 7 days in the week had Fardh = 5.

Weeks are grouped Sun–Sat by default.

Transparency & notes

  • Numbers are a nudge, not a judgement. Sincerity ≫ totals.
  • You can adjust weights later to suit your goals.
  • Future days are disabled; (optionally) you can lock previous days after the next Fajr.
